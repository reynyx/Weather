What does it mean?

Humidity

Measure of moisture in the air, determining how sticky or clammy you might feel.

Dew Point

Temperature at which moisture in the air condenses into water droplets, forming dew.

Feels like temperature

Adjusted temperature perception based on factors like humidity and wind, affecting how warm or cool it seems.

Visibility

Range of clear vision, influenced by weather conditions such as fog, rain, or haze.

Cloud Cover

Extent to which the sky is obscured by clouds, influencing sunlight and overall brightness.

Max UV index

Measurement of the sun's ultraviolet radiation intensity, indicating potential sunburn risk.
